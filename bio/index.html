About Me

I am an experienced electrical engineer with over 10 years of hands-on problem-solving experience in various aspects of electricity grid challenges. Throughout my career, I have been responsible for delivering innovative solutions to complex projects in power system planning and operation. My commitment to integrating my knowledge and skills has enabled me to lead and contribute to groundbreaking projects in the fields of smart grid, renewable energy integration, smart cities, and policy development.

My research interests span across various domains, including:

  • Data-driven demand response utilizing machine learning-based techniques

  • Transactive energy structures, demand response, and peer-to-peer electricity markets

  • Enhancing the resiliency and reliability of energy supply through microgrids

  • Operation and planning of power systems with renewable and distributed energy resources

  • Modeling the interdependent networks in smart cities, such as electricity, natural gas, cloud, and transportation

  • Polynomial optimization and convex relaxation for mathematical problems in energy networks
